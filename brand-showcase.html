Signature motion: layer reveal + light sweep

Homepage hero, scroll-triggered

Two moves, back to back. First the build: 8 bands sweep away bottom-to-top, 480ms each, 40ms stagger. Then the light turns on: the rim flares, a specular highlight sweeps once across the surface, and a soft violet glow rises once before settling — the deliberate reveal described in DESIGN.md.
